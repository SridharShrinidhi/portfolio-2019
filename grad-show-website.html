Graphic Design Grad Show website 2019

Graduation website of the 2019 graduating class of Algonquin College Graphic Design Program.

The Pathfinder website is a digital directory for the graduating class.

Pathfinder

Working on a Vaporwave theme, a representation of the experimental and bold approach of the graduating class, was challenging and learning experience. Working as the deisgn and development lead with a team of designers and developers on the website, initial research on the project involved learning baout our target audience.

Following research, brainstorming wireframes and solving challenges as a team was our priority. Creating a website following a Vaporwave theme, while making the website accessible was challenging.

Creating a responsive, accessible, SEO friendly website for the graduating designers while woring with a team, was a learning experience. To understand and empathize with other ideas while delegating tasks to create a final product that solved our problems helped me understand the dynamics of team work.
